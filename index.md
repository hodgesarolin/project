# Political Research Index

A personal research project exploring current US political actions through historical lens, with comparative analysis of other countries' histories.

## Guiding Principles
- **Facts are sourced** — every claim has provenance
- **Correlation ≠ Causation** — relationships are explicitly labeled
- **Atomic notes** — one concept per note, linked together

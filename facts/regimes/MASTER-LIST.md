## Notes

### Controversial Classifications
Some entries are marked *controversial* because historians debate whether they qualify as dictators/authoritarians:
- **Atatürk** — Authoritarian methods but modernizing reforms
- **Lee Kuan Yew** — "Soft authoritarianism" with rule of law
- **Tito** — Communist but independent, relatively open
- **Nkrumah** — Started democratic, became authoritarian
- **Paul Kagame** — Authoritarian but credited with stability/development

### Categories of Regime Type
- Fascist
- Communist (Marxist-Leninist)
- Military Junta
- Personalist/Strongman
- Absolute Monarchy
- Theocracy
- Single-Party State
- Hybrid/Competitive Authoritarian

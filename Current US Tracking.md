## 2026

### January

#### January 7 - U.S. Withdraws from 66 International Organizations + UNFCCC
- Trump signed presidential memorandum withdrawing from **66 international organizations** (35 non-UN, 31 UN entities)
- Critically: Withdrew from **UNFCCC** (UN Framework Convention on Climate Change) - the 1992 bedrock climate treaty
- U.S. becomes **first country ever** to withdraw from UNFCCC
- Rubio: "We will not continue expending resources...in institutions that are irrelevant to or in conflict with our interests"
- John Kerry called it "a gift to China and a get out of jail free card to countries and polluters"
- **Sources:** [CNN](https://www.cnn.com/2026/01/07/climate/trump-withdrawal-climate-treaty-international-agreements), [Fox News](https://www.foxnews.com/politics/trump-orders-us-withdrawal-from-66-wasteful-global-organizations-sweeping-america-first-crackdown)

#### January 8 - Minnesota National Guard Standoff Developing
- **Walz's warning order:** National Guard on standby (13,000 troops), not yet deployed
- **State Emergency Ops Center:** Activated at Level 2 of 3
- **State Patrol:** 85 Mobile Response Team members deployed to Twin Cities
- **Walz when asked if Guard would deploy against feds:** "Whatever it is: if it's an act of nature, if it's a global pandemic, or in this case, if it is a rogue federal agent. I don't know this time."
- **Walz warning:** "Minnesotans, do not take the bait. Do not allow them to deploy federal troops in the air. Do not allow them to invoke the Insurrection Act. Do not allow them to declare martial law."
- **GOP response - calling for Insurrection Act:**
  - Rep. Mary Miller (R-IL): "Invoke the Insurrection Act. Arrest Tim Walz."
  - Rep. Nancy Mace (R-SC): "What Walz is threatening has a name: insurrection. Mr. President, the law is on your side. Use it."
- **Legal context:** Trump v. Illinois (Dec 23, 2025) - SCOTUS ruled 6-3 Trump lacked authority to federalize state National Guard
- **Walz update scheduled:** 12:30 PM Thursday (Jan 8)
- **Historical significance:** Inverse of civil rights era - state deploying forces to resist federal overreach, not enforce segregation
- **Status:** ESCALATING - Constitutional standoff between state and federal authority

#### January 7 - ICE Kills Renee Nicole Good in Minneapolis
- **What:** ICE agent shot and killed 37-year-old U.S. citizen Renee Nicole Good during immigration enforcement operation
- **Context:** Day after DHS deployed 2,000 agents to Minneapolis (largest operation ever) based on viral right-wing YouTuber video alleging Somali daycare fraud (later mostly debunked)
- **Video shows:** Agents surrounded her car, ordered her out, she reversed then drove forward, agent fired 3 shots to head
- **DHS claim:** She was "weaponizing" her car; Noem called it "domestic terrorism"
- **Local response:** Mayor Frey called DHS narrative "bullshit," told ICE to "get the fuck out of Minneapolis"; Walz warned against "propaganda machine"
- **Who she was:** Mother, poet, ODU grad 2020, was "caring for her neighbors" per city council; mother says she wasn't involved in protests
- **Aftermath:** Schools closed, thousands at vigils, protests ongoing, Walz preparing National Guard, no agent arrested
- **Status:** DEVELOPING - Protests spreading nationwide
- **Jan 8 updates:** FBI took over investigation, kicked out Minnesota BCA; ICE agents showed up at Roosevelt High School; protesters clashed with feds at Whipple Building; AG Ellison says shooting "raises serious questions" and agent "should face charges"
- **See:** [[facts/us/trump-second-term-2025#ICE Killing of Renee Nicole Good (January 7, 2026)]]

#### January 6 - "Coalition of the Willing" Paris Summit on Ukraine
- 35 countries (27 heads of state) met to finalize Ukraine security guarantees
- **First time U.S. participated:** Envoys Steve Witkoff and Jared Kushner attended
- **Paris Declaration agreed:** Binding commitments to support Ukraine if Russia attacks again
- France + UK to lead European peacekeeping force with "military hubs" across Ukraine
- U.S.-led ceasefire monitoring mechanism (drones, sensors, satellites)
- Witkoff: Security protocols "as strong as anyone has ever seen"
- Zelenskyy: "A signal of how seriously Europe and the entire coalition...are ready to work for real security"
- Still unresolved: territorial issues, financing Ukrainian army, Putin hasn't agreed to ceasefire
- **Sources:** [EU Council](https://www.consilium.europa.eu/en/press/press-releases/2026/01/06/robust-security-guarantees-for-a-solid-and-lasting-peace-in-ukraine-statement-of-the-coalition-of-the-willing-issued-by-france/), [NBC News](https://www.nbcnews.com/world/europe/us-backs-security-guarantees-ukraine-summit-kyivs-allies-paris-rcna252737)

#### January 6 - DHS Deploys 2,000 Agents to Minneapolis

# Comparisons

Historical comparisons with explicit relationship types.

## Relationship Types
- **causal** — A likely caused B (requires strong evidence)
- **correlational** — A and B occurred together (no causation implied)
- **temporal** — A and B happened around the same time
- **thematic** — A and B share themes/patterns
- **structural** — A and B have similar structures/mechanisms
- **contrast** — A and B are notably different
